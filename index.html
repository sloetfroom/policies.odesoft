Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Odessoft, we value privacy, security, and the trust of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use any of our products, services, applications, platforms, or websites.

By using our products and services, you acknowledge and agree that certain information may be collected and processed for purposes including account verification, technical support, service delivery, security monitoring, platform improvement, and compliance with applicable legal obligations.

We are committed to handling your information responsibly, transparently, and in accordance with applicable data protection laws. We only collect information that is necessary to operate our services, enhance our products, provide customer support, and maintain a safe and reliable experience for our users.

This Privacy Policy applies to all products, services, applications, platforms, and websites developed, operated, or managed by Odessoft, unless a specific service provides its own separate privacy policy.

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or the way your information is processed, please contact us through our official support or privacy channels.
